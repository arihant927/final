Whether you are a professional model or a new face, test shooting is the best way to exercise your shooting skills. This is a privileged time between you and the photographer to express your character through the power of the image.

Before the shooting, we will be exchanging through emails or phone calls, targeting your goals and expectations. During the shooting I will guide you through my eight years of experience in portraiture to make sure you get the strongest outcome possible.

02

Shooting organisation Shooting organisation

Here is more information regarding the photo shoot session:

Styling:
This is a very important component of the success of a picture. One must bring the styling matching to his or her shooting wishes. The clothes should be ironed properly and well look after while bringing to the shooting location. In case you feel like you don't have the proper styling at home, many stores offer free return policy. Simply buy from them what you need and return later on if you don't wish to keep the clothes.

Make up/hairstylist:
It is possible to shoot without make up/hairstyle artist (women should arrive already wearing make up, with clean and well arranged hair). However, if you wish to have a make up/hairstylist to take care of you during the shooting, it will be possible to arrange a great one for an extra cost of 50 euros.

Timing:
Shooting time takes usually one hour. Cancelling or postponing a shooting session must be made latest the day before the shooting.
